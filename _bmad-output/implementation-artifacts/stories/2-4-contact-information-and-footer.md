# Story 2.4: Contact Information and Footer

**Epic:** Epic 2 - Public Discovery: Landing Page
**Story Key:** 2-4-contact-information-and-footer
**Status:** backlog
**Acceptance Criteria Count:** 2
**Dependencies:** Story 2.1 (Hero Section)

---

## Story

As a **visitor wanting more information**,
I want **to find contact information and site navigation in the footer**,
So that **I can get in touch or navigate to different parts of the platform**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC 1: Contact Information Provided

**Given** I scroll to the footer
**When** the footer loads
**Then** I see:
  - Company email or contact form
  - Social media links
  - Navigation links to key pages
  - Copyright information

### AC 2: Footer is Accessible and Professional

**Given** I'm viewing the footer
**When** I interact with it
**Then** The footer has:
  - Clear layout and typography
  - Working links
  - Professional design
  - Good contrast and accessibility
  - Responsive design

### Frontend Tasks

### Testing Tasks
