## Story

As a **visitor interested in the company**,
I want **to understand the vision and mission of Startup Validator**,

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC 1: Mission Statement Displayed

**Given** I scroll to the mission section
**When** the section loads
**Then** I see:
  - Clear mission statement
  - Company vision for the future
  - Core values (3-5 values)
  - Professional presentation

### AC 2: Section Design is Inspiring

**Given** I'm reading the mission section
**When** I view the content
**Then** The design is inspiring with:
  - Professional typography
  - Engaging layout
  - Subtle animations
  - Strong visual hierarchy
